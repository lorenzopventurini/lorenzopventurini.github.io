About Me

I'm a cofounder and Senior AI Engineer at Fraiya, where I build the AI models that power our products. My background is in medical imaging: I did my DPhil at the University of Oxford working on AI for fetal ultrasound, and before that studied Engineering at the University of Cambridge (MEng, Class I with distinction).

I grew up in Genoa and moved to the UK for university, where I've stayed ever since. These days I'm based in London, splitting my time between building things at Fraiya and the usual suspects: rowing, pub quizzes, and a bit too much cheese.

Always happy to chat about AI, medical imaging, or potential collaborations: drop me a line if you'd like to connect.
